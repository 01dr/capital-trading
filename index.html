About Us

Located in the heart of Southeast Asia, Capital Trading focuses on investing in the most promising and innovative companies. We expedite the early-stage technology startups with revolutionary blockchain projects. As an advisory, we help businesses to choose the most optimal ways to raise capital through tokenization strategies and implement technologies in the right way to push their ideas to the next level.

Capital Trading provides to its partners a full scope of services in the crypto market, which include strategic investments and business advisory services. We build strong connections with our clients by finding the best opportunities for our mutual benefits. Together, we transform global industries by connecting the power of distributed ledger technologies.
